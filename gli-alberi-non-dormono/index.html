Gli alberi non dormono

Trees regulate many of their biological processes through the natural alternation of light and darkness. Artificial light at night can disrupt these rhythms, influencing processes such as growth, seasonal development, and other physiological functions.
Gli alberi non dormono (Trees Don't Sleep) was photographed at night in urban parks, using only existing public lighting. The series explores nocturnal urban landscapes shaped by artificial light, inviting reflection on the fragility of living organisms in urban environments.
