Gli alberi non dormono

Like all living organisms, trees regulate their cycles through the alternation of light and darkness. Light pollution, by interfering with this natural rhythm, can affect processes such as growth and photosynthesis.
Gli alberi non dormono (Trees Don't Sleep) was made at night in urban parks, using only existing public lighting. The series explores nocturnal urban landscapes shaped by artificial light, inviting reflection on the vulnerability of ecosystems exposed to it.
